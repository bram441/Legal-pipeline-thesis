- CRITICAL: Predicates about consequences of actions refer to the ACTOR/SUBJECT (the person who committed the act), not the victim. Map each predicate to the correct entity from the case.

Four weeks, one month, two months, or "a few weeks" are NOT more than four months — assert 'not CausesIncapacityOverFourMonths(entity).' in those cases.
