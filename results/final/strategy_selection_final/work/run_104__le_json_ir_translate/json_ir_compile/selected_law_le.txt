The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires the usufruct of the immovable property that served as the family principal residence at the time the estate of the deceased opened if the estate of the deceased has opened.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires the usufruct of the household effects present in the immovable property that served as the family principal residence at the time the estate of the deceased opened if the estate of the deceased has opened.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires the usufruct referred to in paragraph 1 regardless of which heirs the surviving legally cohabiting partner is called to the estate with.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the usufruct referred to in paragraph 1 if the immovable property that served as the family principal residence is the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.24.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the usufruct referred to in paragraph 1 if the household effects present in the immovable property that served as the family principal residence are the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.24.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the usufruct referred to in paragraph 1 if the immovable property that served as the family principal residence is the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.25.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the usufruct referred to in paragraph 1 if the household effects present in the immovable property that served as the family principal residence are the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.25.
