The surviving spouse acquires the right to the lease of the immovable property if that property served as the main residence of the family at the time the estate of the deceased opened.

The surviving spouse acquires the right to the lease of the immovable property to the exclusion of all other heirs.

The right to the lease of the immovable property is a legal result that arises for the surviving spouse under article 4.20.

The surviving spouse acquires the right to the lease as the sole beneficiary of that right.
