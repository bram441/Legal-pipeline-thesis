A company is a legal entity.

The employee criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if the annual average number of employees of the company is greater than 50.

The turnover criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if the annual net turnover of the company, excluding value added tax, as referred to in article 1:26/1, is greater than 11,250,000 euro.

The balance sheet criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if the balance sheet total of the company is greater than 6,000,000 euro.

More than one small-company criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if the employee criterion and the turnover criterion are exceeded for that company in that financial year.

More than one small-company criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if the employee criterion and the balance sheet criterion are exceeded for that company in that financial year.

More than one small-company criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if the turnover criterion and the balance sheet criterion are exceeded for that company in that financial year.

Not more than one small-company criterion is exceeded for a company in a financial year if more than one small-company criterion is not exceeded for that company in that financial year.

A company is a small company if the company is a legal entity and not more than one small-company criterion is exceeded for the company in the most recently closed financial year on the balance sheet date.

A company is not a small company if more than one small-company criterion is exceeded for the company in the most recently closed financial year on the balance sheet date.
