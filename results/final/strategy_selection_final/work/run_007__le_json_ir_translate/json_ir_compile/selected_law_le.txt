The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires the usufruct of the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence at the time the estate of the deceased opened if the surviving legally cohabiting partner is called to the estate with any heirs.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires the usufruct of the household effects present in the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence at the time the estate of the deceased opened if the surviving legally cohabiting partner is called to the estate with any heirs.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires the usufruct of the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence regardless of which heirs the surviving legally cohabiting partner is called to the estate with.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the right of usufruct over the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence if that immovable property is the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.24.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the right of usufruct over the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence if that immovable property is the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.25.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the right of usufruct over the household effects present in the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence if those household effects are the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.24.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner exercises the right of usufruct over the household effects present in the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence if those household effects are the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.25.
