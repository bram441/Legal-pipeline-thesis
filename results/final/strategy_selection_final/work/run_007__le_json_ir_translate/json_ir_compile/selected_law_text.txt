par. 1. The surviving legally cohabiting partner acquires, regardless of which heirs they are called to the estate with, the usufruct of the immovable property that served as the family's principal residence at the time the estate of the deceased opened, and of the household effects present therein.

The surviving legally cohabiting partner also exercises this right if the goods referred to in the first paragraph are the subject of a right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.24 or in article 4.25.
