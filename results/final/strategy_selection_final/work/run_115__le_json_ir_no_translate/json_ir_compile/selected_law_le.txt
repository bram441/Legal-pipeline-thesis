A company is a micro-company if the company is a small company with legal personality, and the company is not a subsidiary company, and the company is not a parent company, and not more than one micro-company criterion is exceeded for the company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.

The employee criterion is exceeded for a company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year if the annual average number of employees of the company is greater than 10.

The turnover criterion is exceeded for a company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year if the annual net turnover of the company as referred to in article 1:26/1, excluding value added tax, is greater than 900000 euro.

The balance sheet criterion is exceeded for a company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year if the balance sheet total of the company is greater than 450000 euro.

More than one micro-company criterion is exceeded if the employee criterion and the turnover criterion are exceeded for the company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.

More than one micro-company criterion is exceeded if the employee criterion and the balance sheet criterion are exceeded for the company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.

More than one micro-company criterion is exceeded if the turnover criterion and the balance sheet criterion are exceeded for the company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.

Not more than one micro-company criterion is exceeded if more than one micro-company criterion is not exceeded for the company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.
