Art. 1:25.
Micro-companies

par. 1. Micro-companies means: small companies with legal personality that are not a subsidiary or parent company and that on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year do not exceed more than one of the following criteria:
(1) annual average number of employees: 10;
(2) annual net turnover referred to in article 1:26/1, excluding value added tax: 900,000 euros;
(3) balance sheet total: 450,000 euros.

par. 2. Article 1:24, paragraphs 2 to 5 and paragraph 8, shall apply.
