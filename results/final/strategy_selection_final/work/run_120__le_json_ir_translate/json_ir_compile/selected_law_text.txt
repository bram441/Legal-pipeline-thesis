par. 1. Small companies are companies with legal personality that on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year do not exceed more than one of the following criteria:
(1) annual average number of employees: 50;
(2) annual net turnover as referred to in article 1:26/1, exclusive of value added tax: 11,250,000 euros;
(3) balance sheet total: 6,000,000 euros.

par. 2. When more than one of the criteria referred to in paragraph 1 are exceeded or are no longer exceeded, this only has effect when this occurs during two consecutive financial years. The effects shall in that case take effect from the financial year following the financial year during which more than one of the criteria were exceeded or were no longer exceeded for the second time.
