par. 2. When the deceased leaves relatives in the ascending line or close collateral relatives, the surviving spouse acquires:
(1) the full ownership of the share of the first deceased in the common property and in the property held exclusively in undivided co-ownership between the spouses, and
(2) the usufruct of the remaining goods of their own property.
(3) the usufruct of the goods subject to the right of legal reversion referred to in article 4.24 or in article 4.25, unless otherwise provided in the deed of donation or in the will.
