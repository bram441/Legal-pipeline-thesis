A company is a small company if the company has legal personality and not more than one small-company criterion is exceeded on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.

The employee criterion is exceeded for a company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year if the annual average number of employees is greater than 50.

The turnover criterion is exceeded for a company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year if the annual net turnover exclusive of value added tax as referred to in article 1:26/1 is greater than 11,250,000 euros.

The balance sheet criterion is exceeded for a company on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year if the balance sheet total is greater than 6,000,000 euros.

More than one small-company criterion is exceeded if the employee criterion and the turnover criterion are exceeded.

More than one small-company criterion is exceeded if the employee criterion and the balance sheet criterion are exceeded.

More than one small-company criterion is exceeded if the turnover criterion and the balance sheet criterion are exceeded.

Not more than one small-company criterion is exceeded if more than one small-company criterion is not exceeded.

A company is not a small company if the company does not have legal personality.

A company is not a small company if more than one small-company criterion is exceeded on the balance sheet date of the last closed financial year.
