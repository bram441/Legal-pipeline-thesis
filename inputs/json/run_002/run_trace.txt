SECTION II. - INTENTIONAL KILLING, NOT CALLED MANSLAUGHTER, AND INTENTIONAL INFLICTING OF BODILY INJURY.

  Art. 398. Whoever intentionally inflicts wounds or blows shall be punished with imprisonment from eight days to six months and a fine from twenty-six [euros] to one hundred [euros], or with one of those penalties alone.
  In case the offender acted with premeditation, he shall be sentenced to imprisonment from one month to one year and a fine from fifty [euros] to two hundred [euros].

  Art. 399. If the blows or wounds result in illness or inability to perform personal labor, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from two months to two years and a fine from fifty [euros] to two hundred [euros].
  The offender shall be punished with imprisonment from six months to three years and a fine from one hundred [euros] to five hundred [euros], if he has acted with premeditation.

  Art. 400. The punishment is imprisonment from two years to five years and a fine from two hundred [euros] to five hundred [euros], if the blows or wounds cause an incurable appearing disease, inability to perform personal labor for more than four months, complete loss of use of an organ, or a severe mutilation.
  The punishment is detention from five years to ten years, in case the offender has acted with premeditation.

  Art. 401. When the blows or wounds are intentionally inflicted, but without the purpose to kill, and nevertheless cause death, the offender shall be punished with detention from five years to ten years.
  He shall be punished with detention from ten years to fifteen years if he commits those acts of violence with premeditation.
