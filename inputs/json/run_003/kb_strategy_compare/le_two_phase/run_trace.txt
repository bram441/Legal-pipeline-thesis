Art. 400. The penalty is detention from five years to ten years in case the offender has acted with premeditation.

Art. 401. When the blows or wounds are intentionally inflicted but without the aim to kill, and nevertheless cause death, the offender shall be punished with detention from five years to ten years.
He shall be punished with detention from ten years to fifteen years if he commits those acts of violence with premeditation.
