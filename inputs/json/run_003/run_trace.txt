Art. 400. Art. 401. When the blows or wounds are inflicted intentionally, but without the intent to kill, and yet cause death, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from five years to ten years.

Art. 398. Whoever intentionally inflicts wounds or blows shall be punished with imprisonment from eight days to six months and a fine of twenty-six [euro] to one hundred [euro], or with one of these penalties alone.

Art. 399. If the blows or wounds result in illness or incapacity to perform personal labor, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from two months to two years and a fine of fifty [euro] to two hundred [euro].

Art. 400. The penalty shall be imprisonment from two years to five years and a fine of two hundred [euro] to five hundred [euro], if the blows or wounds result in a disease that appears incurable, incapacity to perform personal labor for more than four months, the complete loss of use of an organ, or a severe disfigurement.
The penalty shall be imprisonment from five years to ten years if the offender acted with premeditation.

Art. 401. When the blows or wounds are inflicted intentionally, but without the intent to kill, and yet cause death, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from five years to ten years.
