=== LAW (plain text input) ===
SECTION II. - INTENTIONAL KILLING, NOT CALLED MANSLAUGHTER, AND INTENTIONAL INFLICTION OF BODILY INJURY.

  Art. 398. Whoever intentionally inflicts injuries or blows shall be punished with imprisonment from eight days to six months and a fine from twenty-six [euros] to one hundred [euros] or with one of these penalties only. <Law 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>
  In case the culprit acted with premeditation, he shall be sentenced to imprisonment from one month to one year and a fine from fifty [euros] to two hundred [euros]. <Law 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>

  Art. 399. If the blows or injuries result in illness or incapacity to perform personal labor, the culprit shall be punished with imprisonment from two months to two years and a fine from fifty [euros] to two hundred [euros]. <Law 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>
  The culprit shall be punished with imprisonment from six months to three years and a fine from one hundred [euros] to five hundred [euros] if he acted with premeditation. <Law 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>

  Art. 400. (See NOTE 1 under TITLE) The penalty is imprisonment from two years to five years and a fine from two hundred [euros] to five hundred [euros], if the blows or injuries cause either an apparently incurable disease, or [1 incapacity to perform personal labor of more than four months]1, or the complete loss of use of an organ, or a severe disfigurement. <Law 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>
  The penalty is (detention from five years to ten years) if the culprit acted with premeditation. <Law 2003-01-23/42, art. 66, 041; Effective date: 13-03-2003>
  ----------
  (1)<Law 2016-02-05/11, art. 20, 114; Effective date: 29-02-2016>

  Art. 401. (See NOTE 1 under TITLE) <Law 2003-01-23/42, art. 67, 041; Effective date: 13-03-2003> When the blows or injuries are intentionally inflicted, but without the intent to kill, and nevertheless cause death, the culprit shall be punished with detention from five years to ten years.
  He shall be punished with detention from ten years to fifteen years if he commits such acts of violence with premeditation.
