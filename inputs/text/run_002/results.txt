=== LAW (plain text input) ===
SECTION II. - INTENTIONAL KILLING, NOT CALLED MANSLAUGHTER, AND INTENTIONAL INFLICTING OF BODILY INJURY.

Art. 398. Whoever intentionally causes wounds or blows shall be punished with imprisonment from eight days to six months and a fine from twenty-six [euro] to one hundred [euro], or with one of these penalties only. <W 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>
If the offender acted with premeditation, he shall be sentenced to imprisonment from one month to one year and a fine from fifty [euro] to two hundred [euro]. <W 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>

Art. 399. If the blows or wounds result in an illness or incapacity to perform personal labor, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from two months to two years and a fine from fifty [euro] to two hundred [euro]. <W 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>
The offender shall be punished with imprisonment from six months to three years and a fine from one hundred [euro] to five hundred [euro], if he acted with premeditation. <W 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>

Art. 400. (See NOTE 1 under TITLE) The penalty is imprisonment from two years to five years and a fine from two hundred [euro] to five hundred [euro], if the blows or wounds result in either an apparently incurable disease, or an incapacity to perform personal labor of more than four months, or the complete loss of the use of an organ, or a serious mutilation. <W 2000-06-26/42, art. 2, Effective date: 01-01-2002>
The penalty is (imprisonment from five years to ten years), in case the offender acted with premeditation. <W 2003-01-23/42, art. 66, 041; Effective date: 13-03-2003>
----------
(1)<W 2016-02-05/11, art. 20, 114; Effective date: 29-02-2016>

Art. 401. (See NOTE 1 under TITLE) <W 2003-01-23/42, art. 67, 041; Effective date: 13-03-2003> When the blows or wounds are intentionally inflicted, but without the purpose of killing, and nevertheless cause death, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment from five years to ten years.
He shall be punished with imprisonment from ten years to fifteen years if he commits those violent acts with premeditation.
